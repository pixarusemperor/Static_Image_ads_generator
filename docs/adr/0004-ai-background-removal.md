# 0004. AI Background Removal for Subject Extraction

We evaluated manual cropping, automated background removal, and AI bounding box clipping. We decided to use automated AI background removal (utilizing the open-source `rembg` library or an API) to automatically isolate subjects (people/products) as transparent PNG assets when uploaded. Users can manually adjust the scale, rotation, and crop frames in the HTML/CSS Editor, balancing ease-of-use with professional direct-response marketing design aesthetics.
